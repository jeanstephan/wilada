The Second Birth

Liberation in the Four Gospels
A spiritual study in the narratives of liberation and the teachings of Christ
"If the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed"
John 8:36
In the beginning was freedom

Jesus Christ did not come to establish another system, nor to replace one chain with another. He came — and this is the unbelievable news — to proclaim radical liberation for the human being from everything that stands between them and their original image.

In these pages you will find a contemplation of how every sermon Jesus preached, every parable he told, every healing he performed — was opening a closed door, or restoring to the person their stolen right to be what they were created to be: a free child in their Father's home.

The Gospel at its core is not a book of laws but a book of liberation.
